2014-2015 Executive Board

Jasleen Bains, Co-founder and President

I am passionate about human rights. I aspire to increase youth involvement in pressing global issues, because we are the future. The world depends on youth to lead these efforts with innovation and local projects that have global impact. Volunteering for global goals facilitates a cosmopolitan culture that is necessary for international human rights standards and active communities.
